🔒 WhatsLog Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 4, 2026

Summary: WhatsLog respects your privacy. We do not collect, store, or share your personal data. All processing happens locally in your browser.

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

WhatsLog is designed with privacy at its core. We explicitly do NOT collect or store:

  • Your WhatsApp messages or chat content
  • Your contacts or contact information
  • Your Google account credentials
  • Personal identification information
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • IP addresses or location data

2. How WhatsLog Works

WhatsLog operates entirely within your browser using Chrome's extension APIs:

  • Local Processing: All message extraction and data processing happens locally on your device
  • No Server Storage: We do not have any backend servers that store your data
  • Direct Export: When you export to Google Sheets, data goes directly from your browser to your Google account via Google's official APIs
  • No Third-Party Sharing: Your data is never shared with any third parties

3. Permissions Explained

WhatsLog requests certain Chrome permissions to function. Here's why each is needed:

web.whatsapp.com Access

Required to inject the export panel into WhatsApp Web interface and extract message data that you choose to export.

Google OAuth & Sheets API

Required to authenticate with your Google account and create spreadsheets in your Google Drive when you choose to export to Google Sheets.

Storage Permission

Used to save your extension preferences (like default export format) locally in your browser.

Downloads Permission

Required to save CSV/Excel files to your computer when you export.

4. Data Security

  • All data processing occurs within your browser's secure sandbox
  • No data is transmitted to our servers (we don't have any)
  • Google authentication uses industry-standard OAuth 2.0 protocol
  • Export files are saved directly to your chosen location

5. Your Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • Choose which chats to export
  • Select date ranges for export
  • Delete exported files at any time
  • Revoke Google permissions from your Google Account settings
  • Uninstall the extension to remove all local data

6. Third-Party Services

WhatsLog interacts with the following third-party services on your behalf:

  • WhatsApp Web: Owned by Meta. Subject to WhatsApp's Terms of Service.
  • Google Sheets API: Owned by Google. Subject to Google's Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

8. Compliance

WhatsLog is designed to comply with:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Chrome Web Store Developer Program Policies
